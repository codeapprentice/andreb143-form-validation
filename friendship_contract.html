Description

As a result of the outcry for order and preservation of the sacred bonds of friendship, this webpage was created. There are some who need a guiding hand to help them navigate the thin line between friend and enemy. Much like those who came before and lost their memory of the good times due to nonsense stemming from simple occurances, such as women fighting over who saw that hot guy first, to eating the last chip, to which superhero is better Batman or Superman (To which, I say there is no contest. Superman wins everytime, hands down.).
This will help to put an end to the silly fights that breakup childhood friends and family members across the world... or maybe it will make things worse, either way it will give some guidance to make things seem a little easier to maneuver.
